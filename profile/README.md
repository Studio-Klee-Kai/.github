# Studio Klee Kai

## Who are we?
Studio Klee Kai is a cooperative studio centered of passionate developpers and artists. We're centered around our members, offering everybody an equal voice under our 1 member 1 vote policy. We chose the cooperative model to go against the current standard in the gaming industry, where workers are disposable and do not share in the success of their work.

## Our mission
Our goal is to make games, plain and simple. Our goal is to be able to live off of making games that are both fun for us to work on and for you to play.
